Welcome to Synethsize, a game where the music is cool and the points don't matter! Synthesize imports your playlists from Spotify and assigns each song a color based on its audio features. The game will play a preview of each song and you will guess which color represents each song in your playlist. Colors are based on the HSV color model and are calculated accordingly:

  • Hue: An average of the song's key + valence + energy + loudness + danceability + mode
  • Saturation: An average of the song's loudness + energy
  • Value/Brightness: valence + danceability + tempo
